About the App

Arch Nest is an innovative app designed to benefit both architects and new homeowners.It allows homeowners to easily
find the best architects in their local area, shortlist two candidates, and finalize one. Once an architect is selected, homeowners can request a house plan, for which the app will charge a fee. The drafted plan will then be converted into a 3D simulation, enabling users to visualize their house before construction begins. Additionally, the app can estimate the overall budget for the house before construction is completed. It also provides a feature to order construction materials based on the user's budget and quality preferences.
